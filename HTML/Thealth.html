About Us

Telehealth

24/7 urgent care and online doctors available

Get convenient, high-quality virtual care — from everyday health needs to urgent and mental health support. No waiting rooms, no hassle — just fast, expert care when you need it most.

How it works

Book Appointment

Book your appointment on MediGo in just a few clicks—choose your doctor, pick a time, and get instant confirmation. Stay on track with automated reminders and easy rescheduling options.

Share symptoms

Share your symptoms easily on MediGo to help doctors understand your needs faster. Get accurate guidance and the right care without unnecessary delays.

Consult Online

Consult online with MediGo and connect to trusted doctors from the comfort of your home. Get quick, secure, and reliable medical advice anytime you need it.

Get Prescription

Get your prescription instantly on MediGo after consultation. Access medications safely and conveniently without extra clinic visits.

We take your concerns seriously

Telehealth consultations with MedIGo are a great way to get care when you need it — especially between visits to your regular GP. Our caring doctors will take the time to understand your condition and provide the right treatment to help you stay well. Your medical information is completely confidential and will never be shared without your permission. Telehealth is a safe and affordable way to manage common health issues from the comfort of home
