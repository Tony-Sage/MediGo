Frequently
Asked Questions

What condition can i treat with telehealth?

You can treat minor illnesses like flu, allergies, skin rashes, and manage chronic conditions such as diabetes and hypertension via telehealth

How do i prepare for my consultation?

To prepare for your consultation on the MediGo app, ensure you are in a quiet, private space with a stable internet connection. Have your medical history, current medications, and any symptoms written down beforehand. Join the call a few minutes early, and make sure your device’s camera and microphone are working properly. If applicable, keep any recent test results nearby to discuss with your doctor.

Is my privacy protected?

Yes, your privacy is fully protected. All consultations on the MediGo app are encrypted and comply with healthcare privacy regulations. Your medical information is stored securely and will never be shared without your explicit consent. We are committed to keeping your personal and health data confidential at all times.

How do I get a prescription?

After your online consultation, if the doctor determines that a prescription is appropriate, it will be sent to you digitally through the MediGo app. You can download it directly or have it forwarded to your preferred pharmacy for fulfillment. All prescriptions are issued by licensed medical professionals and follow local regulations.
